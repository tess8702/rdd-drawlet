How Maximum Class Size Rules Affect Class Sizes and Earnings

Instructions

Click to start. Move the mouse to draw the line. When you are done, double click to stop.

It is a canvas defined by x and y axis. The size of school enrollment is on the x axis, ranging from 0 to 120; and the size of a classroom on the y axis, from 0 to 35. There is a verticl line at school enrollment equals 30,60,90, and 120, repectively. Therefore, the canvas has four panels, school enrollment 0-30, school enrollment 30-60, school enrollment 60-90, and school enrollment 90-120. There are three buttons below the canvas, which would enable users to clear the drawing or to start over, and submit the answer when it is done.
